(Coding the Past)

For this class, we had to make a Layered protrait of or Interviewee using photoshop. The images in the portrait were based on things mentioned in the interview.

The Portrait was based off of our interview in History class.
We then had to write a reflection on the portrait. The Reflection was in MLA format, and had to descriptivly explain the main points in the portrait

We also had to write an essay. The topic was based off of anything that was mentioned in the interview. I wrote about cars, specifically their history and impact, as they were as mentioned in the inteview. Click on any of the essay pages to view it in Google Docs
